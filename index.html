Everything About Me

Read Me! Read Me! *This site is a work in progress*

Hello and welcome! My name is Matthew, after graduating with a BS in Psychology from Brigham Young University in December of 2023, I worked as a Registered Behavioral Technician. The work was fulfilling but not what I wanted to do as a career. After leaving that I kept myself busy working with a friend who owns his own carpet cleaning business, this gave my mind time to think about what I wanted to do, and the difference I'd like to make in the world. Starting back in fall of 2025 I returned to school to complete the necessary prerequisites for medical school. I'm currently enrolled and working towards those now at Utah Valley University.

Along with this new found drive for getting into medical school and becoming a psychiatrist, I have many other interests, hobbies, and projects that I hope to work on and develop. Hence the creation of this site as a place to show those and to have some accountability for the things that I start. As you see in the sidebar there are a few things I'm working on, please check them out! Below this I'd like to highlight some of the hobbies and interests I have.

Computers and Video Games

Computers have always been an interest to me since I was young, and by extension of that video games. I remember recieving an old thinkpad and playing Age of Empires II (probably the best RTS of all time!) for hours and with family. Skyrim was the game that hooked me more than anything else, most of my highschool free time that wasn't taken up by school, sports, or family and friends was spent on the PS3 playing Skyrim. The desire to play modded Skyrim is actually what got me interested in building my own gaming computer. It wasn't until after working a summer in college that I was able to build my first real PC.

I start with Gaming and Computers because that is where a lot of my other hobbies stem from, self hosted AI, digital media server, minecraft server, game development, 3D modeling, 3D printing, Linux, and FOSS.
